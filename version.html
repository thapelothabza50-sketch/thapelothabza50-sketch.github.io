Our Vision 2026

The Central Hub for Student Growth in SA

We are dedicated to helping over 10,000 students at UMP and beyond. By expanding our network and offering free resources, we make student life easier and more accessible.

National Growth

Each year we add more universities and colleges, building strong relationships with accommodation providers to expand our free student support network.

Empowering Students

Campus Collective is a free hub where students can apply for varsity, find safe housing, and grow through our community-led platform.

Entrepreneurship

Our Campus Shop allows students to create free accounts to sell goods and services, helping them stay financially sustainable while they study.
