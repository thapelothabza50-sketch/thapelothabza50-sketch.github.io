Residence Information

View In offers modern, secure, and fully catered living spaces tailored for first-year and returning students. We focus on a supportive community environment, ensuring a smooth transition into university life.

  • Location: Entokozweni
  • Facilities: High-speed Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, common study areas, and a recreation lounge.
  • Security: 24-hour security, and CCTV monitoring.
  • Other info: Each room has its own private bathroom.
