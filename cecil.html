Residence Information

Cecil offers modern, secure, and fully catered living spaces tailored for first-year students. We focus on a supportive community environment, ensuring a smooth transition into university life. Our location provides easy access to all lecture halls and the main library.

  • Room Types: Single and Shared Rooms available.
  • Catering: Three meals daily (Monday to Sunday) included in the fees.
  • Facilities: High-speed Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, common study areas, and a recreation lounge.
  • Security: 24-hour security, biometric access control, and CCTV monitoring.
