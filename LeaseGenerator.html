2.1 Rent is payable monthly by NSFAS on behalf of the student. [cite: 282, 417] 2.2 The Lessor confirms that no deposit, admin fees, or utility joining fees have been charged to the student. 4. SECURITY & ACCESS POLICY

4.1 Access Control: The Lessor shall provide the Lessee with biometric or key access to the Premises. Sharing of access credentials is a material breach. [cite: 265, 421]

4.2 Emergency Access: The Lessor may enter the premises without notice in cases of emergency (fire, flood, medical) or with 24-hour notice for routine maintenance. [cite: 325, 333]

4.3 Visitor Policy: No overnight visitors are permitted without prior written consent. All guests must adhere to building security protocols. [cite: 266, 307]

5. PROPERTY PROTECTION

5.1 The Lessee shall not make any structural alterations, paint, or drive nails into walls without consent. [cite: 323] 5.2 Damage beyond fair wear and tear caused by the Lessee will be for the Lessee's account, provided it is documented in an inspection report. [cite: 315]

6. NOISE & CONDUCT POLICY

6.1 Quiet Hours: Strict quiet hours are observed from 22:00 to 07:00 daily. Noise levels must be conducive to an academic environment at all times.

6.2 Substance Abuse: The use, sale, or distribution of illegal substances on the property is strictly prohibited and will lead to immediate lease cancellation. [cite: 303]

6.3 Sub-letting: Sub-letting of the room or sharing the unit with unlisted individuals is strictly forbidden and results in the forfeiture of NSFAS funding. [cite: 289, 425]

7. TERMINATION

7.1 This lease terminates automatically if the Lessee ceases to be a registered student at a Higher Education Institution. [cite: 395] 7.2 One month's written notice is required by either party for early termination. [cite: 400]
